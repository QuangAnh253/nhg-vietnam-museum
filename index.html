buildings
No.1 Trang Tien, Hoan Kiem This building presents Vietnam’s history from prehistoric times to the end of the Nguyen dynasty in 1945. Designed in Indochinese architectural style, it houses rich collections of artifacts from the Đông Sơn culture, Champa civilization, and various feudal dynasties, offering a deep insight into early Vietnamese heritage.
buildings
216 Tran Quang Khai, Hoan Kiem Originally constructed in 1917 as the Indochina Department of Trade, this building covers Vietnam's history from the mid-19th century to the present. It features exhibits on the country's struggles for independence, the Vietnam War, and post-war reconstruction, including gifts to President Ho Chi Minh and the Communist Party.
